The Technician

When Tony Maricano, a humble supply tech working the night shift, stops a violent hospital break-in, his hidden instincts as a fighter are revealed. The patient he saves — retired detective Harry Potts — draws him into a covert war against The Queen Bees, a powerful matriarchal cartel trafficking a designer drug disguised as candy.
